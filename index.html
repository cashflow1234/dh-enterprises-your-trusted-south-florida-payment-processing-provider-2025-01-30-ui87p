Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you support?

We support all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay), contactless payments, and ACH transfers. Our system is regularly updated to accommodate new payment technologies.

How quickly can I start processing payments?

Most merchants can be fully operational within 24-48 hours after application approval. Our streamlined setup process includes same-day equipment delivery and installation, plus comprehensive staff training.

What security measures do you have in place?

We maintain PCI Level 1 compliance and employ end-to-end encryption, tokenization, fraud detection algorithms, and 24/7 monitoring. Our security protocols exceed industry standards to protect your business and customers.

Are there any long-term contracts required?

No. We believe in earning your business every day. Our services are month-to-month with no early termination fees. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your service at any time without penalties.

What kind of support do you offer?

You'll have access to 24/7 technical support, a dedicated account manager, and our local South Florida team. We provide ongoing training, quarterly account reviews, and emergency on-site assistance when needed.
